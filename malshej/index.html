WELCOME TO MALSHEJ LAKE CAMPING!

Step into the playground of nature. Discover a beautiful, blissful destination away from the noise and pollution of the city. Easily accessible from Pune and Mumbai, Malshej Lake Camping is a thoughtfully planned camp site for enthusiastic nature lovers like you. Breathe in the cool, fresh air; enjoy the captivating views; experience the tranquility and feel pampered by our heartwarming hospitality. We offer you a secure, comfortable Tent Stay along with hygienically prepared tasty food (veg and non-veg). You also get to participate in a number of exciting outdoor activities, where you will find both adventure and fun. So take the much-deserved break from the boredom and stress of city life. Simply grab you rucksack and head straight to Malshej Lake Camping. A great outdoors experience indeed awaits you!

LOCATION

The moment you step in at Malshej Lake Camping, you know you should have visited much earlier!Nestled in the lap of the Sahyadri ranges, our camp site is surrounded by rolling hills carpeted with greenery. The natural beauty and superb tranquility of the place will make your worries melt away, making you feel exuberant and active.

It is truly a handpicked location where nature rules supreme; a selected place where time comes to a standstill. Situated only 110 Km from Pune and Mumbai, Malshej Lake Camping is a conveniently located weekend destination for people of all age groups and interests.

YOUR HEALTH SAFETY, OUR TOP PRIORITY

The well-being of our guests is paramount to us. In view of the prevailing pandemic situation, we are taking all possible precautions to safeguard your health.

Our key safety protocols include:

Sanitization at entry point
