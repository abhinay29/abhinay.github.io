All About One Bite

Our Founder Saurabh Shah, a true foodie and health conscious person, has travelled across the globe. What he always found missing in the country was the range of healthy snacks the country has. We have been importing many brands in the country for potato chips for the love of taste, however he wondered, what if the taste and health combined.

He founded One Bite with the intention to provide the country with a range of healthy and tasty snacks. These snacks are roasted and empower the local farmer group. Best quality wheat is procured from organic farms in India and used for One Bite products. The flavours are brought by keeping in mind a wide range of audience including Vegans, Jains, Vegetarians, the Chat lovers, youngsters, working mums, health conscious parents and many others.

Our Main aim is to provide a healthy range of snack and empower the local farmers and workers.
